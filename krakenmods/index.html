OpenGOAL Modding is possible

Refined and retuned gameplay elements for Jak II and Jak 3.

What started as a few changes to Jak II's traffic manager grew into a full-scale passion project. Thanks to OpenGOAL, KrakenMODS rebuilds and rebalances gameplay systems across the original Naughty Dog trilogy, 20 years later.

Custom gameplay, tested in motion.

Combat, traversal, traffic, and encounter tuning are treated as connected systems instead of isolated edits.

The Problem

How do you figure out an old codebase with little to no documentation, while still making changes that feel good in actual gameplay?

OpenGOAL

GOAL was a revolutionary engine built by Naughty Dog for their respective games. OpenGOAL brings that engine to PC, making deeper study, testing, and modding possible.

The modding journey is the documentation.

OpenGOAL made the original Jak and Daxter trilogy playable on PC. KrakenMODS builds on that foundation through my careful experimentation.
