Ambition at it's Maximum

The biggest passion project I've ever made: featuring custom gun modifiers, a full skill & leveling system, custom soundtrack, and an epic climactic ending to the custom story featured in my previous mods.

Challenging Difficulty

Turns Jak 3's easier difficulty and ramps it up in all missions. All weapons are obtained at a random order from enemy drops.

Skill System

Jak has a main level, and 2 others for his guns and vehicles. Enemies also have a level, making certain areas very dangerous to traverse.

Looting System

Enemies drop a 3-tiered crate when they die. You can obtain collectables, super gun mods, pets, and many more.
